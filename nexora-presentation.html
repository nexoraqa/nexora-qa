The Workflow
4 Steps.
The Entire Loop
Automated.
From the moment a QA submits an evaluation to the moment an agent acknowledges their coaching — everything runs automatically. No manual follow-up. No dropped balls.
1
QA Assessor evaluates the call
NC / CC / CB scoring with full sub-attribute breakdown. Live score preview. Review step before submitting.
2
Coaching auto-created and sent to TL
If errors found, a coaching item is created instantly. QA adds comment. Team Lead is notified immediately.
3
Team Lead accepts or disputes
TL reviews the coaching. Accept → moves to agent. Dispute → returns to QA with comment. Full trail kept.
4
Agent acknowledges — loop closes
Agent reads the full coaching note. One tap to acknowledge. Status → Closed. Audit trail saved forever.
